DESCRIPTION

You play as Chomper, a voracious character with a mission to devour all the pellets scattered throughout the map. The goal is simple: eat all the pellets while avoiding the relentless pursuit of the ghosts who want to catch you. Use your strategic wits and quick reflexes to outmaneuver the spectral foes and obtain a high score.

HOW TO PLAY

Use the "W", "A", "S", and "D" keys on the keyboard to control Chomper and make him devour the pellets, once all the pellets have been consumed the map will reset with more and the ghosts will be quicker.

The game ends when you lose all your lives, which happens when you collide with the ghosts.

Eat the power up located in the middle of the map and gain the ability to eat ghosts for a brief period!
